About This App

The Problem

In modern healthcare, patients often undergo repeated imaging procedures such as CT scans and X-rays. This happens because imaging data is fragmented across different hospitals and systems.

As a result, patients may receive unnecessary radiation exposure, and healthcare systems face increased costs.

Our Solution

This app provides a patient-centered "Imaging Passport" that allows users to store and manage their medical imaging history in one place.

By scanning QR codes from imaging procedures, patients can build a complete, accessible record of their scans.

Our Goal

The goal is to improve transparency, reduce unnecessary repeat imaging, and increase patient awareness of radiation exposure.

In the future, this system could support better clinical decision-making and reduce overall healthcare costs.
